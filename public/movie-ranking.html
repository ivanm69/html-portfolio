The Best Movies According to Ivan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Five Nights at Freddy s (2014)

Five Nights at Freddy's is an American horror thriller film based on the popular video game series of the same name. The film tells the story of a security guard named Mike Schmidt who takes on a night shift at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, a family-friendly restaurant where animatronics come to life at night and attack anyone they see. The film was praised for its faithfulness to the source material and its creepy atmosphere. 5.5/10

Cuban Fury (2014)

Cuban Fury is a British comedy-drama film about two friends, Bruce and Terry, who reunite after years apart to train for a salsa dancing competition. The film was praised for its humor, heart, and performances by Frost and Pegg. 10/10

V for Vendetta (2006)

V for Vendetta is a dystopian thriller film set in a future where the United Kingdom has become a totalitarian state. The film follows V, a masked vigilante who fights against the government's oppression. The film was praised for its visuals, action sequences, and political themes. 9/10
